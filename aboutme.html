About Me

Hi! My name is Gabriela Betancourth Rodrigues, I’m 24 years old, and I’m currently studying Applied Computer Science(ACS) at Thomas More Geel. As a developer with a background in Graphic Design and Marketing & Advertising, I’m passionate about blending creativity with technology to create meaningful digital experiences.

My journey began with curiosity and has evolved through my university studies, where I’m deepening my skills in programming languages and different frameworks.

Why I Chose ACS?

  • The flexibility of the field lets me explore diverse technologies and creative approaches.
  • Its mix of problem-solving and creativity matches my interests and encourages constant growth.
  • My design and marketing background sparked my tech curiosity, and each new concept deepens it.

Applied Computer Science is a journey that challenges me to think in new ways, continuously pushing me forward.
